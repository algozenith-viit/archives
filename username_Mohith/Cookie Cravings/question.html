Cookie cravings

You love the cookies that are chocolate flavoured meanwhile your friend likes the cookies which are vanilla flavoured on a fine day, you both visit a bakery shop near by. The baker cooks four types of cookies!
1) Chocolate flavoured cookies (You like the most!, your friend wont like it!)
2) Vanilla flavoured cookies (You hate the most!, your friend likes it!)
3) Chocolate and Vanilla flavoured cookies (Both of you like it!)
4) Plain cookies (Both of you hate it!)
The baker is some emotional, That he will stop baking the cookies if one of you leaves the shop. Initally the intresent of cookies is 0 for both of you. You can increase you interest by picking the cookies you like and decrease the interest by picking the cookies you hate. one of you will leave the shop if the interest on the cookies is less than 0. Find the maximum number of cookies you and your friend can eat.
Note : The cookie that is liked by both can be shared and their intrestes on cookies increases by one for both, but in other cases if one like the cookie, then the person who likes the cookie will increase intresent on cookie other's decrease their interest! If both dont like a particular cookie then both interests decreases by one. Only the cookies which both of you like and which both of you don't like can be shared.
